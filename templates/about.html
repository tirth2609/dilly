The Beginning

Dilly's Veg Kitchen was founded in 2003 by the Sharma family, who had a vision to create a vegetarian restaurant that would redefine how people experience meat-free cuisine. What started as a small 20-seat eatery has grown into one of the most beloved vegetarian dining destinations in the city.

Our founder, Chef Nikhil Sharma, brought with him decades of experience in vegetarian cooking from his travels across India, Southeast Asia, and the Mediterranean. His philosophy was simple: vegetarian food should never be a compromise but rather a celebration of flavors, textures, and creativity.

"The true art of vegetarian cooking lies not in imitating non-vegetarian dishes, but in celebrating the incredible diversity and richness of plant-based ingredients."

Chef Nikhil Sharma, Founder of Dilly's Veg Kitchen

Today, Dilly's Veg Kitchen continues this legacy, combining traditional recipes with modern culinary techniques to create memorable dining experiences for our guests.

Our Culinary Philosophy

The principles that guide every dish we create

Fresh & Seasonal

We source the freshest seasonal produce to ensure that our dishes showcase ingredients at their peak flavor. Our partnerships with local farmers allow us to maintain the highest quality standards.

Traditional Techniques

We honor traditional cooking methods that have been perfected over generations. From slow-simmering curries to hand-rolling breads, we believe that authentic techniques are essential to vegetarian cooking.

Innovative Approach

While respecting tradition, we continually innovate with new ingredients, techniques, and presentations. Our chefs regularly experiment to create vegetarian dishes that surprise and delight our guests.

Our Ingredients

At Dilly's Veg Kitchen, we believe that great vegetarian food starts with exceptional ingredients. Here's how we ensure that every dish meets our high standards:

  • Local Sourcing: We partner with local farmers to source fresh produce, reducing food miles and supporting our community.
  • Organic Focus: Whenever possible, we use organic ingredients to ensure the purest flavors and minimize environmental impact.
  • Seasonal Menus: Our menu evolves with the seasons, highlighting the best ingredients available throughout the year.
  • Housemade Specialties: From our signature spice blends to freshly baked breads, we prepare many ingredients in-house for unmatched quality.

This commitment to quality ingredients is the foundation of our culinary approach and a key reason why our customers keep coming back for more.
